The Brandeis University Quant Club is a chartered club at Brandeis University [v. 2023-3.1]


Who we are

Founded in 2021, the Quant Club is a society dedicated to the education and training of students in applied mathematics and computer science for applications in quantitative finance, academia, sports analytics, and economics. In many ways, this club is purpose-built to teach the quantitative skills necessary to approach abstract problems that require complex, non-linear decision making.

You will:

  • Gain a real-world understanding of machine learning applications and their various limitations.

  • Have the opportunity to build large-scale object oriented applications in JavaScript, Python, C++, and or Java.

  • Get introduced to advanced quantitative finance topics, including but not limited to numerical methods, probability, and stochastic processes.

  • Meet like-minded Brandeis students and faculty to grow your academic and career interests.

Our projects

Have you ever wanted to build a program that can guess the answer to a Wordle? Or maybe a Python library that can perform advanced optimization techniques?

Throughout club sessions, members get the opportunity to work on individual research-oriented projects and present to the group on their findings, as well as any difficulties or trouble they may be having. For projects requiring public equity/derivative related data, the club provides a myriad of premium data endpoints to our active members, as well as support and funding in finding all necessary resources to succeed.
